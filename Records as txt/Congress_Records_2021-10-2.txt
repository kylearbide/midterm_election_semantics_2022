MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE 
At 1:32 p.m., a message from the 
House of Representatives, delivered by

lege cost as a barrier to every student 
having access to a well-prepared and 
diverse educator workforce, and for 
other purposes.

SURFACE TRANSPORTATION 
EXTENSION ACT OF 2021 
Mr. WYDEN. Mr. President, I ask 
unanimous consent that the Senate 
proceed to the immediate consider-
ation of H.R. 5434, which is at the desk. 
The PRESIDENT pro tempore. The 
clerk will report the bill by title. 
The senior assistant legislative clerk 
read as follows: 
A bill (H.R. 5434) to provide an extension of 
Federal-aid highway, highway safety, and 
transit programs, and for other purposes. 
There being no objection, the Senate 
proceeded to consider the bill. 
Mr. WYDEN. Mr. President, I ask 
unanimous consent that the bill be 
considered read a third time and 
passed, and that the motion to recon-
sider be considered made and laid upon 
the table. 
The PRESIDENT pro tempore. With-
out objection, it is so ordered. 
The bill (H.R. 5434) was ordered to a 
third reading, was read the third time, 
and passed. 
ORDERS FOR MONDAY, OCTOBER 4, 
2021 
Mr. WYDEN. Mr. President, I ask 
unanimous consent that when the Sen-
ate completes its business today, it ad-

ness, the Senate resume consideration 
of the House message that accompanies 
S. 1301. 
The PRESIDENT pro tempore. With-
out objection, the request of the distin-
guished senior Senator from Oregon is 
agreed to.
